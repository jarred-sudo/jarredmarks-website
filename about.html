About the Firm

Jarred Marks

Jarred Marks is a strategic attorney focused on delivering clear and practical legal counsel in Utah. Before practicing law, he spent nearly a decade in Aerospace & Defense, supporting over $5 billion in complex agreements — experience that shaped a disciplined, high-stakes approach to negotiation, regulatory compliance, and risk management.

A former Division I athlete at Xavier University, Jarred brings the same preparation and competitive focus to his clients' matters. His practice encompasses civil and commercial litigation, estate litigation and probate, corporate transactions, and real property, with an emphasis on practical, results-driven solutions.

As a fully virtual firm, Jarred Marks Law keeps overhead low and passes those savings on — so clients get sophisticated counsel at competitive rates, with direct access to their attorney.
